1. Intro

In medieval science and philosophy, the word "demon" was ascribed to any phenomenon that was bizarre, unexplainable, or imperceptible.

Inspired by this age-old curiosity for the unknown, we named our first product to express this desire to discover and harness the nearly imperceptible forces that surround us in everyday life.

2. Does it Demon?

The Demon Box interacts with EMF to generate audio, MIDI, and CV.

It represents an alchemy for the 21st century, revealing the unheard tones of electromagnetic signatures in objects all around us.

To use the Demon Box:

1. Power via USB-C

2. Connect an instrument cable to any output.

3. Run through effects, then into a speaker.

4. Take a power tool, old radio, or electronic ephemera, and wave it above the inductors on the top of the Demon Box.

What sounds will you discover?

3. MIDI & CV

The Demon Box generates MIDI both CC messages and note data.

Create gestural, performative MIDI scales as notes are generated by interacting with the inductors or by using the audio inputs.

You can use the MIDI hardware port or USB-C port.

The Demon Box also outputs CV, or control voltage.

Connect this to a modular synth for envelope generators and real-time parameter shifts.

Connect this to a video synth and watch the patterns take on their own life.

4. About ER

Eternal Research is an electronic instrument design studio dedicated to revealing the hidden music in everyday objects and providing synesthetic audiovisual tools to creators.

Understanding the symbiotic relationship between art and technology, Eternal Researchs products reflect an alchemy for the 21st century, channeling overlooked and untapped electromagnetic forces to the hands of newcomers and seasoned artists alike.
